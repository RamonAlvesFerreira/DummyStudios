About

Protocol Null stands out as an engaging cyberpunk-themed tower defense game. With creative direction led by Ramon Alves, co-founder of Dummy Studios, this game aims to set a new standard in the genre. Get ready for its anticipated release in the fourth quarter of 2023, exclusively for PC Steam. Prepare to immerse yourself in a universe where strategy meets cyberpunk style, delivering a distinct and thrilling gaming experience.

History

In E-City, dominated by mega corporations, the rebellion of androids led by DICE confronts the oppression of Actavision. The story unfolds after a successful uprising of self-aware androids battling the company that created them, exploring themes of android rights and social conflicts. Two new androids, K-12 and Z4, join DICE but are captured and hacked by Actavision. Sent on a risky mission, they face obstacles and dangers to save their kind, culminating in a fight to destroy a factory and secure android freedom.

Gameplay

Protocol Null is an electrifying cyberpunk action game. Players take on the role of K-12 and Z4, tasked with protecting the vital STE vehicle across the bridge, facing enemies along the way.

K-12 brings strength, explosive abilities and mechanical knowledge, while Z4 excels in agility and precision combat. With intriguing class options like Engineer, Tank, Weapons Specialist, or Medic, each character offers a distinct play style. The arsenal, which ranges from pistols to swords, machine guns and shotguns, caters to varied preferences.

About

Save the Homeland is an immersive 2D run-and-gun game featuring charming pixel art. Directed by Erick Eduardo and Ramon Alves, co-founders of Dummy Studios, the game was released for free on the Gamejolt platform on April 30, 2020.

History

Amidst a communist uprising led by the U.F.C.N, war unfolds, and F.S.C soldiers are called upon to combat the Union's attack. Follow these brave fighters as they enter the chaotic streets to defend their nation. Slay their enemies, and uphold peace.

Gameplay

Save The Homeland is a 2D shooting game of the run-and-gun genre, released for free in April 2020 on the Gamejolt platform. In the game, you take on the role of one of three characters of your choice to cross enemy lines. You can choose to play as Gustavo, who wields a heavy yet slow arsenal; Vitor, a hunter who uses long-range precision weapons; or Caio, the most balanced character with rapid gunfire. Get ready for an exciting journey through this action-packed 2D world full of challenges.

About

Bloodloss is a first-person shooter with an enigmatic and sinister atmosphere. Directed by Ramon Alves, co-founder of Dummy Studios, the game has a free demo version on the Gamejolt platform.

History

Daniel, seeking revenge, sets off for Hyde Mansion, but inexplicably, the cultists in the residence remain unharmed by the bullets from his weapon. Consequently, Daniel meets his demise, and upon arriving in hell, Satan offers him redemption, which, unfortunately, would come at a very high cost.

Gameplay

In Bloodloss, you will face crucial choices where Daniel's blood serves as both special ammunition to defeat enemies and his key to survival, requiring careful management. Unraveling puzzles is essential to obtain his primary weapon and enter the mansion, while his post-hell vision reveals a world in shades of gray, except for the red of blood, making exploration of the mansion's lifeless surroundings a unique and sinister experience. Get ready for a journey where redemption and survival intertwine under the relentless pursuit of destiny.
