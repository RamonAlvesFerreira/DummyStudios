History

In E-City, dominated by mega corporations, the rebellion of androids led by DICE confronts the oppression of Actavision. The story unfolds after a successful uprising of self-aware androids battling the company that created them, exploring themes of android rights and social conflicts. Two new androids, K-12 and Z4, join DICE but are captured and hacked by Actavision. Sent on a risky mission, they face obstacles and dangers to save their kind, culminating in a fight to destroy a factory and secure android freedom.

Gameplay

Protocol Null is an electrifying cyberpunk action game. Players take on the role of K-12 and Z4, tasked with protecting the vital STE vehicle across the bridge, facing enemies along the way.

K-12 brings strength, explosive abilities and mechanical knowledge, while Z4 excels in agility and precision combat. With intriguing class options like Engineer, Tank, Weapons Specialist, or Medic, each character offers a distinct play style. The arsenal, which ranges from pistols to swords, machine guns and shotguns, caters to varied preferences.
